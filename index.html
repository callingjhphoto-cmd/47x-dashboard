Regulatory Intelligence
EU AI Act — High-Risk Obligations
The EU AI Act's "high-risk" classification obligations activate August 2026. Any AI system used for credit scoring, employment decisions, or critical infrastructure management in EU jurisdictions will require: (1) conformity assessments, (2) human oversight documentation, (3) audit trails. The 47X scoring engine is positioned as a white-box, explainable alternative to black-box AI tools — a significant compliance advantage.
Source: EUR-Lex, European Commission AI Office
